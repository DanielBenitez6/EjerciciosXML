XML Syntax Examples

1. Element named test with content

<test>This is our first element!</test>

2. Hierarchy of book element with subelements

<book>
    <chapters>
        <!-- chapter elements could go here -->
    </chapters>
    <author>
        <name>John Doe</name>
        <address>123 Fiction Street, Novel City</address>
    </author>
</book>

3. Element entry with two attributes

<entry no="24" date="27.10.2004" />

Or with nested content:

<entry no="24" date="27.10.2004">
    <!-- content goes here -->
</entry>
